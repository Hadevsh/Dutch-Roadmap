#### Expressing Likes and Preferences
üí¨ There are 3 main ways to say you like something in Dutch:
| Expression            | Literal Meaning            | Use it when‚Ä¶                                      |
|-----------------------|----------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| _houden van_          | to love                    | You like/love something deeply (strong preference) |
| _leuk vinden_         | to find something nice     | You find something enjoyable/pleasant             |
| _graag doen / eten_   | to like doing/eating       | You enjoy doing something (used with verbs)       |

#### 1. _Houden van_ (To love / like strongly)
- Used to express **affection** or strong preference.
- Always followed by a noun or pronoun, often with _ik hou van..._

**Examples:**
-   *Ik hou van muziek.* ‚Äì I love music.  
-   *Zij houdt van haar hond.* ‚Äì She loves her dog.  
-   *Wij houden van reizen.* ‚Äì We love to travel.
> Note: ‚Äú*Hou*‚Äù is the short form of *houden* used in daily speech: *Ik hou van...* = I like/love...

#### 2. _Leuk vinden_ (To find something nice/enjoyable)
- Used to express **mild enjoyment**.
- Always in the structure: *[Subject] + vindt/vinden + [noun] + leuk*

**Examples:**
-   *Ik vind voetbal leuk.* ‚Äì I like football.  
-   *Hij vindt haar leuk.* ‚Äì He likes her.  
-   *We vinden het leuk om samen te koken.* ‚Äì We like cooking together.

#### 3. _Graag doen/hebben/eten_ (To like doing/having/eating)
-   Used with **verbs**: indicates that you **like doing** something.
-   Word *graag* comes **after the verb**.

**Examples:**
-   *Ik lees graag boeken.* ‚Äì I like reading books.  
-   *Zij eet graag pasta.* ‚Äì She likes eating pasta.  
-   *Wij sporten graag in het park.* ‚Äì We enjoy exercising in the park.

#### Expressing Dislikes
To express dislike, you use **_niet_ (not)** or **_geen_ (no)** with the verbs above.

**Examples:**
-   *Ik hou niet van kaas.* ‚Äì I don‚Äôt like cheese.  
-   *Zij vindt voetbal niet leuk.* ‚Äì She doesn‚Äôt like football.  
-   *Wij koken niet graag.* ‚Äì We don‚Äôt enjoy cooking.
